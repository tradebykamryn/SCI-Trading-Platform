📐 Strategy Lab

SELECT YOUR STRATEGY before analyzing any trade. The ⭐ ICC ⭐ strategy is your core method — always use this first. Use Strategy Lab OUTSIDE of trading hours to build and backtest new ideas with Lona. During a live session, focus only on ICC.
Build a Strategy
CORE STRATEGY
⭐ ICC ⭐
Enter during the Continuation phase — after a swing high/low breaks (Indication), price pulls back (Correction), then continues in the original direction. The Continuation phase IS your entry signal. This is the SCI method.
When to use: Every single trade. Start here always.
Breakout Retest
Price breaks a key level, returns to retest it from the other side, then continues. Enter on the retest confirmation.
When to use: After a clear indication break with strong momentum.
Session Open Play
Trade the initial momentum at the London open (2AM) or NY open (9:30AM) when institutional volume enters the market.
When to use: When a clear trend is established and the session is opening.
Trend Following
Trade only in the direction of the 4H trend. Enter on pullbacks to Higher Lows (uptrend) or Lower Highs (downtrend).
When to use: Strong trending markets with clear HH/HL or LH/LL patterns.
Counter-trend Setup
Trade against the current trend when a reversal pattern appears. Higher risk — requires strong multi-timeframe confirmation.
When to use: Advanced only. Requires reversal signals on multiple timeframes.
Custom
Build a completely custom strategy with your own rules and test it.
When to use: When you have a specific idea to document and test.

⚠️ SCI RISK RULES:
• Maximum 1-2% risk per trade — never more
• Stop goes BELOW the last Higher Low (longs) or ABOVE last Lower High (shorts)
• Give stops room to breathe — no tight stops
• Always confirm minimum 1:3 Risk/Reward before entering
• If R/R is less than 1:3 — skip the trade, no exceptions

📚 SCI Playbook

REFERENCE PAGE — Check here before any trade if you are unsure about a rule. The Quick Reference card is always visible at the top. Do not trade if you are unsure — the rules are here for a reason. Read this during your learning phase, not during a live session.

⚡ SCI Core Rules — Always On Screen

📈 Trend Rules

HH + HL = BULL/UPTREND → BUY from Higher Lows ONLY
LH + LL = BEAR/DOWNTREND → SELL from Lower Highs ONLY
Equal highs + equal lows = RANGE → STAY OUT
❌ NEVER buy at a Higher High — wait for the pullback (HL will form)
❌ NEVER sell at a Lower Low — wait for the pullback (LH will form)

⏰ Session & Risk

🇬🇧 London: 2:00 AM – 6:00 AM EST
🇺🇸 New York: 8:30 AM – 12:00 PM EST ⭐ PRIMARY
Prime entry: 9:30 AM EST (NY market open)
❌ NEVER trade the Asian session
Minimum 1:3 Risk/Reward on every trade
Stop BELOW swing low (longs) / ABOVE swing high (shorts)
Pivot lookback: 5 (beginner) → 10 (advanced)

🤝 THE TEAM RULE

Multiple Timeframes Match = TEAM = Trade is valid.
Timeframes do NOT match = NO TEAM = Do NOT trade.

4H = Identify the trend (overall direction)
1H = Monitor the correction (watch for it to end)
15M = Execute the entry (find exact entry point)

All three must agree or you do not enter. No exceptions.
