📐 Strategy Lab

Build a Strategy
⭐ ICC ⭐
Enter during the continuation phase — after a swing high/low is broken (indication), price pulls back (correction), and price then continues in the original direction. The continuation phase IS your entry signal. This is the highest probability SCI setup.
Breakout Retest
Price breaks a key level, returns to retest it from the other side, then continues. Enter on the retest confirmation.
Session Open Play
Trade the initial momentum at the open of London (2AM) or New York (8:30AM) sessions when institutional volume enters.
Trend Following
Trade in the direction of the higher timeframe trend only. Enter on pullbacks to structure in an established HH/HL or LH/LL sequence.
Counter-trend Setup
Trade against the current trend when a reversal pattern appears. Higher risk — requires strong confirmation and smaller position size.
Custom
Build a completely custom strategy using your own rules. Fill in all fields manually.
